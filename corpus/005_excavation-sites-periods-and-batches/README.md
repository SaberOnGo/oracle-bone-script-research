# Excavation Sites, Periods, And Batches / 出土地点、时代与批次

English:
This directory will store period, site, pit, batch, collection, and provenance records.

Current registers:

- `000_collection-registers/001_institutional-collection-provenance-staging.csv`: institutional collection-level provenance staging records extracted from official library, museum, and archive pages.

The collection staging rows preserve source-level provenance facts such as holding counts, digitized-record counts, named collection provenance, excavation context, and institutional crosswalk notes. They do not replace object-level catalog records.

简体中文：
本目录将保存时代、地点、坑位、批次、收藏和出处记录。

当前索引：

- `000_collection-registers/001_institutional-collection-provenance-staging.csv`：从官方图书馆、博物馆和档案数据库页面派生的机构馆藏级出处暂存记录。

馆藏暂存行保存来源级出处事实，例如馆藏数量、数字化记录数量、具名收藏来源、出土语境和机构 crosswalk 说明。它们不能替代对象级著录记录。
