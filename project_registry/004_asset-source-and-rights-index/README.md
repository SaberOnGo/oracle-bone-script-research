# Asset Source And Rights Index / 资产来源与权利索引

English:
Track image, rubbing, hand-copy, screenshot, and downloaded-asset provenance here. Rights-unclear assets may be committed when research needs them, but the asset index must show source provenance, rights status, and risk notes.

Single files should stay under `SIZE_LIMIT = 30 MiB`. If a research asset must exceed that limit, record it in `003_size-limit-exceptions.csv`; files at or above 40 MiB must not be committed.

简体中文：
在此追踪图片、拓片、摹本、截图和下载资产的来源。研究需要时可以提交权利不明确的资产，但资产索引必须显示来源、权利状态和风险提示。

单个文件原则上应低于 `SIZE_LIMIT = 30 MiB`。如果研究资产必须超过该限制，应写入 `003_size-limit-exceptions.csv`；达到或超过 40 MiB 的文件不得提交。
