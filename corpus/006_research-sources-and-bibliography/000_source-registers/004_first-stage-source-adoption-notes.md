# First-stage Source Adoption Notes / 第一阶段来源采纳说明

English:
The first-stage source policy is strict: adopt professional, institutional, museum, library, national, international, or peer-reviewed research sources; exclude general news sites, entertainment sites, unsourced popular pages, and unaudited hobbyist collections from the formal corpus.

Adopted core professional sources:

- `src-xiaoxuetang-jiaguwen`: primary external character-head and glyph-form reference.
- `src-xiaoxuetang-obm`: primary Heji material-source and inscription provenance reference.
- `src-ihp-oracle-rubbings`: authoritative rubbing and collection-number provenance source.
- `src-ihp-museum-oracle-bones`: official museum collection reference for selected artifacts.
- `src-yinqi-wenyuan`: adopted professional discovery and cross-reference source, with rights caution because the platform disclaimer reports mixed third-party material.

Large research datasets are useful but not primary philological authority by themselves. `src-hust-obc` is important because it targets 1,588 deciphered and 9,411 undeciphered oracle-bone character classes. `src-obimd` is important because it preserves image-level, character-level, sentence-level and reading-order information. Both should be handled through the large-source workflow before raw image download.

简体中文：
第一阶段来源政策必须严格：采纳专业学术机构、博物馆、图书馆、国家级/国际级机构和同行评审研究来源；一般新闻网站、娱乐网站、无来源科普页面和未经审计的民间整理集合，不进入正式语料。

已采纳的核心专业来源：

- `src-xiaoxuetang-jiaguwen`：甲骨单字字头和字形来源的首选外部引用。
- `src-xiaoxuetang-obm`：《甲骨文合集》材料来源和卜辞出处的首选引用。
- `src-ihp-oracle-rubbings`：拓片和馆藏号的权威出处来源。
- `src-ihp-museum-oracle-bones`：史语所博物馆选定藏品的官方来源。
- `src-yinqi-wenyuan`：正式采纳为专业发现源和交叉引用源；但因平台声明包含第三方材料，必须保留权利风险提示。

大型研究数据集很有价值，但不能单独作为文字学释读权威。`src-hust-obc` 重要，因为它覆盖 1,588 个已释字和 9,411 个未释字图像类别。`src-obimd` 重要，因为它包含图像级、字符级、句级和阅读顺序信息。两者的原始图片下载前都必须走大型来源处理流程。
